## Why This Paper Matters

The Transformer changed the default architecture for many NLP systems by replacing recurrent sequence modeling with attention-based computation.

## Core Idea

Self-attention lets the model compare tokens directly across a sequence, while positional encodings preserve order information.

## Questions To Revisit

- How does attention behave on short, noisy text such as headlines?
- Which parts of the architecture matter most for small datasets?
- What implementation details are most important when reproducing results?
